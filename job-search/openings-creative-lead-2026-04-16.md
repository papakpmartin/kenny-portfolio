### Why These Roles Are a Good Fit for Kenny:

1. **Creative Lead** 
   - Perfect bridge from Senior Creative Account Manager
   - Values client management + creative execution
   - Your portfolio shows strong campaign work
   - Salary range: $85K-$120K in MSP

2. **Associate Creative Director (ACD)**
   - Stretch role but appropriate with 4 years at Adtaxi
   - Typically requires ~5-7 years experience
   - Focus on execution over full CD strategy
   - Salary range: $95K-$130K in MSP

3. **Senior Art Director**
   - Direct path from current role
   - Adobe Creative Suite skills directly applicable
   - Strong portfolio is key differentiator
   - Salary range: $80K-$110K in MSP

4. **Senior Creative Manager**
   - Combines account management + creative oversight
   - Your dual skillset is perfect fit
   - Often client-facing role
   - Salary range: $85K-$115K in MSP
